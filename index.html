FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly will I see results from Meta or Google Ads?
Initial data starts coming in within 3–7 days of launch. Meaningful optimization requires 4–6 weeks — this is the time algorithms need to learn your audience and for creative testing to produce statistically significant results. Month 2+ is typically when you start seeing consistent, scalable performance.
What ad budget do I need?
The minimum recommended budget is $1,000/month paid directly to the platform. This gives the algorithm enough data to optimize properly. For faster results and more testing, $2,000–$5,000/month is ideal. The management fee (my fee) is separate and does not include ad spend.
Do you work with e-commerce or lead generation businesses?
Both. E-commerce brands focused on ROAS and revenue, and service businesses (lawyers, dentists, contractors, coaches, SaaS) focused on lead volume and cost per lead. The strategy differs significantly between the two, and I tailor everything to your specific business model.
Is there a long-term contract?
No. All packages are month-to-month. You can cancel with 7 days' notice before the next billing period. I don't lock clients into contracts — I keep them by delivering results.
Will I have access to my own ad account?
Always. I work inside your ad account — not a separate agency account. You have 100% access and ownership. If we ever stop working together, all your campaigns, data, and history stays with you.
Do you create ad creatives?
Yes, basic static creatives are included in Growth and Scale packages (4 and 6 creatives/month respectively). I also adapt your existing materials for ad placements. Professional video production is not included, but I can provide a clear creative brief and direction for your team or a videographer.
What makes your approach different from an agency?
You work directly with me — not an account manager who reports to a strategist who reports to someone else. I manage a small number of clients at any given time, so each account gets daily attention. No onboarding fees. No inflated overhead. Just focused, data-driven campaign management.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us understand how you interact with our site and improve your experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

Required for the website to function. These cannot be disabled. They do not collect personal data.

Analytics Cookies

  • Google Analytics 4: Tracks page views, session duration, and traffic sources. Data is anonymized. Retention: 26 months.
  • Hotjar (optional): Records user behavior for UX improvement. You can opt out at hotjar.com/opt-out.

Advertising / Tracking Cookies

  • Meta Pixel: Tracks website visitors from Meta ads. Used for campaign optimization and remarketing. See Meta's data policy.
  • Google Ads Tag: Tracks conversions from Google Ads. Used for bid optimization.

Preference Cookies

Remember your choices (e.g., cookie consent preference). Stored locally for 12 months.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling analytics cookies won't affect site functionality but may limit our ability to improve the service. To opt out of Google Analytics tracking, install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
